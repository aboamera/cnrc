About the Company

The National Research Centre established the NRC Company, and the General Authority for Investment and Free Zones approved the issuance of the incorporation certificate of the NRC Innovative Products Company as an Egyptian joint stock company No. 2001 RA, after reviewing NRC Board Resolution No. 192 approving the establishment of an Egyptian joint stock company under the name "NRC Innovative Products Company". This was in implementation of the Law on Incentives for Science, Technology, and Innovation, which allows universities and research centers to establish companies.
